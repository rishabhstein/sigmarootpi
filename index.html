Rishabh P. Sharma



I am a Ph.D. student of Physics in University of Warsaw. My PhD research is about pattern formation in dissolving rocks. In carbonate rocks, a reactant such as acid rain, in certain physical conditions, can form highly conductive flow channels known as wormholes—not the ones related to Einstein's general relativity theory. In nature, these patterns are known as karst formations whose examples are caves, solution pipes and dendrites
