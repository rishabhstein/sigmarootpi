Swami Vivekananda

A famous student of Rama Krishna Paramhansa. I have read two books of him, one is his memoirs and second is about Yoga which is classified in four categories:

  • Karma Yoga
  • Bhakti Yoga
  • Raj Yoga
  • Jnana Yoga

Recommended:

  • Autobiography, Swami Vivekananda on Himself
  • A collection of his lectures on different Yoga philosophy. The Complete Book of Yoga: Karma Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, Raja Yoga, Jnana Yoga
