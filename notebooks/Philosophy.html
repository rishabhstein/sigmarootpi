Philosophy


by Rishabh P. Sharma sigmarootpi.com

What is philosophy and what is it useful for? The answer to this question changed with time. In old times, it was a guide to live an ideal life [1] which is also evident in Indian philosophies as well as Roman philosophies such as Stoicism and Cynicism. Nevertheless, in modern times, it became a medium of intellectual pleasure, resulting in the limited usage of it in daily life.
