Cooking

There are abundance of food recipes but among few the ones I have tried are following.

  1. **Masala Tea:**For one cup, first boil the water and add freshly crused Cardamom, Ginger and Black pepper. After the boil, add a tea spoon of black tea which will give a second boil. Now add the milk and keep the temperature uniform by stirring with spoon. Wait for a couple of boil and tea is ready to be served.
  2. Daal Tadka: It's a recipe of lentil curry in which two type of lentils---moong (green lentil) and arhar (pigeon pea lentil) daal---are used. Moong gives base to this recipes along with the texture of arhar. For masala, I usually prefer tomato based masala
  3. Saboot Masoor: Soak the masoor for 15 minutes then cook it in pressure cooker with traditional tomato masala with one whistle at high flame and 5 whisles at medium flame.
  4. Aloo Jeera: Boil aloo in pressure cooker then prepare masala with a bit more cumin
