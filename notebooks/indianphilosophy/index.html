Indian Philosophy

There are so many of them which were practiced in ancient times, unfortunately not any more. With my slow progress the few I found---thanks to books---are following:

  • Ajivikism
  • Buddhism
  • Jainism
  • Charvaka

To read:

  • Chandradhar Sharma Guleri, A Critical Survey of Indian Philosophy
  • Harendra Prasad Sinha, Bharatiya Darshan Ki Rooprekha
  • Rabindranath Tagore, Sadhana: The realisation of life
  • Nisargadatta Maharaj, I am that
  • Ramkrishna Bhattacharya, Charvaka Fragments: A New Collection
  • Jeaneane Fowler, Perspectives of reality : an introduction to the philosophy
