Meditations: Marcus Aurelius


by Rishabh P. Sharma sigmarootpi.com

Around 2000 BC years old journal of one of the most powerful king of Roman empire. Full of inspiring entries (Quotes). There are a few which, ironically, still are so applicable. A few, I like:

  1. You could leave right now....
    Let that determine what you do and say and think.

    —Book Two, P.20
  2. No random actions...
    None not based on underlying principles.

    —Book Four, P.01
