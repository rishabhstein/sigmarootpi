Ajivika or Ajivikism—as west pronounces it—is an Indian philosophy of Nastika founded by some guy Goshala who believed in Niyati or Fatalism. I found this reference in a book, The Discovery of India, by Nehru but Wiki says the sources can be found in Buddhism and Jainism.
