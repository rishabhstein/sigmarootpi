  • Interesting ideas of "ending" of Sindhu Ghati civilization die to Aryan immigration.

  • The people from Paniya civilization might extinct due to Aryans.

  • After some time Vedas become part of supersitition.

  • This leads to writing of Vedanta and exploration of different philiosophies which are cited in this book. Some of the main philosophies cited in the book, are:
